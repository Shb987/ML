Crop Disease Prediction

The Plant Disease Detection and Classification project aims to develop a system capable of identifying diseases in plants through image analysis. With the increasing demand for efficient agricultural practices and the challenges posed by plant diseases, automated systems for disease detection offer a promising solution to farmers and agronomists. This project leverages deep learning techniques to analyze images of plant leaves and accurately classify them into healthy or diseased categories. By providing farmers with timely and accurate information about the health status of their crops, the system aims to facilitate early disease detection, prompt treatment, and ultimately improve crop yield and reduce economic losses.
